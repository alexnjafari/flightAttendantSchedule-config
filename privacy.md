# Privacy Policy — Crew Status

Effective date: July 14, 2026

Crew Status (“the App”) helps flight attendants share schedule status with family using a private share code. This policy explains what information the App uses and why.

## Information we process

**On your device**
- Schedule details you upload (from CCS screenshots or photos), including airports, times, trip numbers, and flight numbers
- Your display name (optional) for sharing status with family
- App preferences and cached schedule data stored locally on your device

**When you share with family**
- Your display name, trip schedule details, and share code are sent to our private share server so family members with your code can view your status
- Screenshots and camera photos stay on your device; only parsed schedule text is shared, not the images themselves

**When viewing live flight status**
- Flight number and route details may be sent to a flight-status data provider (via our server) to show delay, gate, and related status
- This lookup does not require an account

**Family (viewer) mode**
- Entering a share code fetches the shared schedule for that code from our share server

## Camera and Photos

The App requests camera and photo library access only so you can capture or select CCS schedule screenshots. Images are processed on device for schedule text extraction. We do not upload those images to our servers.

## How we use information

- To show your trip timeline and live status in the App
- To sync schedule status to family members who have your private share code
- To look up live flight status for flights on your schedule

## Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Schedule details are shared only with people who have your private share code, and with service providers that power sharing and flight-status lookups (for example our Cloudflare share worker and flight-status API).

## Data retention

Shared schedule data remains available while sharing is enabled for your code. You can stop sharing in the App, which removes the ability for new viewers to follow that code. Local data remains on your device until you delete the App or clear trips.

## Children’s privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13.
